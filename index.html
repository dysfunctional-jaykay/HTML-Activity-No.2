✔ Overview

SMCC-CCIS is an annual campus event that highlights student projects, industry talks, and hands-on workshops about software development, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and more.

Join us for a week full of learning, networking, and fun as we celebrate the power of technology in education & innovation.
